# User Manual - Digital Saver Smartwatch

## Getting Started

### Charging the Watch
1. Connect the USB cable to the charging port on the watch
2. The charging indicator will light up (red = charging, green = full)
3. Charging time: approximately 2-3 hours

### Wearing the Watch
1. Thread the band through the case
2. Secure with the clasp
3. Wear on the inside of your wrist for better PPG readings
4. Ensure the sensor is in contact with your skin

### Initial Setup
1. **Download the app** from the app store (or build from source)
2. **Enable Bluetooth** and location permissions
3. **Open the app** and search for "DigitalSaver Watch"
4. **Tap to connect** and follow the prompts
5. **Add emergency contacts** in the app settings

## Using the Watch

### Viewing Health Data
The watch displays:
- Heart rate (BPM)
- Estimated blood pressure (mmHg)
- Battery level
- Connection status

### Manual Emergency Alert
Press and hold the button for 3 seconds to trigger a manual emergency alert.

### Automatic Alerts
The watch automatically detects:
- **Fall Detection**: Impact after free-fall (possible unconsciousness)
- **Arrhythmia**: Irregular heartbeat patterns
- **Hypertension**: Blood pressure above threshold

## App Features

### Home Screen
- Real-time health metrics display
- Connection status
- Quick emergency button
- Access to history and settings

### Emergency Contacts
1. Open Settings → Emergency Contacts
2. Tap + to add a contact
3. Enter name, phone number, relationship
4. Mark one contact as "Primary"

### History
View past health readings organized by:
- Today
- This Week
- This Month

### Settings
- **Language**: Change app language (10 languages supported)
- **Dark Mode**: Toggle dark/light theme
- **Notifications**: Enable/disable alerts
- **Thresholds**: Customize when alerts trigger

## Battery Life

- **Continuous monitoring**: 2-3 days
- **With optimizations**: 5-7 days
- **Standby**: 2+ weeks

Tips to extend battery life:
- Reduce display brightness
- Disable unnecessary notifications
- Use sleep mode when not needed

## Troubleshooting

### Watch won't turn on
- Charge the battery
- Check the power connection
- Press and hold the button for 5 seconds

### No data showing on app
1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled
2. Check that watch is connected
3. Restart both watch and app
4. Check that the watch sensor is on your skin

### Inaccurate readings
- Ensure the watch is snug on your wrist
- Keep the sensor clean
- Avoid movement during measurement
- Wait 30 seconds for stable reading

### Emergency alert not working
- Check that emergency contacts are configured
- Verify phone has SMS permissions
- Ensure location services are enabled

## Care and Maintenance

### Cleaning
- Wipe with a soft, damp cloth
- Do not submerge in water
- Avoid harsh chemicals

### Storage
- Keep in a cool, dry place
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Store with some charge (40-60%)

### Do Not
- Disassemble the watch
- Use if damaged
- Expose to water beyond IP67 rating
- Use non-approved chargers

## Safety Information

### Intended Use
Digital Saver is a wellness device intended for:
- General health monitoring
- Fitness tracking
- Emergency alert notifications

### Not For
- Medical diagnosis
- Clinical monitoring
- Life-critical applications

### Important Notes
- This is NOT a medical device
- Do not ignore symptoms
- Consult healthcare professionals
- In emergencies, call local emergency services

## Emergency Information

### When Alert Triggers
1. Stay calm
2. The watch will vibrate and beep
3. If you're okay, tap "I'm Okay" in the app
4. If you need help, the app will call/SMS your emergency contacts

### Emergency Numbers
**Egypt**: 123 (General Emergency)
**EU**: 112
**USA**: 911

Update your emergency contacts for your region.
